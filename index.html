Privacy Policy — Networthy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

Networthy is developed by Ten Dimension Labs Pte. Ltd. ("we," "us," "our"). This policy explains what data Networthy collects, where it lives, and who — if anyone — it's shared with.

The short version: Networthy has no backend server. Your financial data — transactions, accounts, balances, net worth — is stored only on your device. We never see it, store it on our own servers, or sell it.

Data stored on your device

Everything you enter into Networthy — brokerage transactions, account balances, categories, watchlist tickers, notes — is stored in a local database on your device only. This data is never transmitted to us, because we don't operate a server for the app to send it to.

Uninstalling the app permanently deletes this data. We have no copy and cannot recover it for you.

Live market data

To show current and historical prices, dividends, and ticker information, the app sends ticker symbols you're tracking to Yahoo Finance's public market data service. This is limited to the ticker symbols themselves — no other information about you or your holdings is included in these requests.

Optional Google Drive backup

If you choose to sign in with Google and enable Drive backup, a copy of your local database is uploaded to your own Google Drive account — not to us. We never have access to this backup; it's stored entirely under your Google account, subject to Google's own privacy policy. This feature is off by default and only activates if you explicitly sign in and enable it.

Purchases

Plus and Pro unlocks, and any donation, are processed entirely through Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment card details — Google handles all payment information directly. We only receive confirmation of which product was purchased, which is used to unlock the corresponding features in the app.

What we don't do

  • No advertising, and no ad networks embedded in the app
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No sale or sharing of your data with third parties
  • No account required to use the app's core features

Children's privacy

Networthy is not directed at children and is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the "last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to admin@tendimensionlabs.com.
